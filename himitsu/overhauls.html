Overhauls

BLEED Overhauls how damage and force is applied, making combat much more unforgiving and closer to classic Fallout. Crippled limbs on crit, burning and bleeding effects and more Main File - BLEED
JIP LN Fonfig Customizes some settings in JIP LN to make the game more dynamic and challenging, for example by distributing weapon mods to NPCs Install from archive and place under Fonfig separator
SawyerBatty An edited version of the famous JSawyer mod by FNV's director Josh Sawyer. This edit by RoyBatty reverts changes to carry weight, level cap & Logan's Loophole. It also fixes many bugs, and achieves parity with TTW. The pre-order packs are placed in logical areas around Springvale and Megaton when you start in DC Main File - SawyerBatty
Three Perk Bounty Upon level up you will be presented with a choice of three randomly selected perks from the available perk pool. You may re-roll the random selection up to three times, but this carries a chance of giving you an unlucky perk until next level Main File - Three Perk Bounty
TTW Quick Start Skips the boring Vault 101 intro and starts the game immediately in the cave just outside the vault. The Medicine Bobblehead is not given at the start, you can pick it up later during Trouble on the Homefront
